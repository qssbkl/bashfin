GOT7 consists of 7 members: JB, Mark, Jackson, Jinyoung, Youngjae, BamBam and Yugyeom. Debuted on 16th January 2014, under JYP Entertainment. (Credits: Kprofiles)

GOT7 originated from Seoul, South Korea. This bands song genre are K-pop and hip-hop. They started their career in 2014, and still ongoing up until now.

As a famous boyband, they have several labels, including JYP Entertainment, Epic Records and Sony BEC-TERO Music. 
This group is also associated with JJ Project and JYP Nation, as some of their members originated from those fellow associations.
